About the text message program

By opting in you consent to receive SMS text messages from Curb Crews related to your account and service.

What you will receive: service reminders, sign-up and service confirmations, schedule changes, and account and billing notifications.

Sample messages:

  • "Curb Crews: Hi Jane, reminder that your trash pickup is tomorrow 07/18. We'll have your bins at the curb the night before and returned by 6pm. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help."
  • "Curb Crews: Thanks for signing up! Your bin valet service starts 07/21 on your Monday collection day. Manage your account at https://curbcrews.com. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help."

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out: reply STOP to any message at any time. For help: reply HELP, email hello@curbcrews.com, or visit curbcrews.com.

Mobile opt-in information and consent are never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
