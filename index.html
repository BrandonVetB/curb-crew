FAQ

The questions everyone asks.

What if I'm on vacation?

Pause your plan from your account or send us a text. We stop service and your billing pauses too. Unpause whenever you're back.

What if my pickup day changes?

Just let us know, or update it in your account. We sync to your city's schedule, including holiday shifts, so your cans always go out on the right night.

How do I cancel?

Two taps in your account, or one text. No contract, no cancellation fee, no awkward phone call. You can come back anytime.

What areas do you serve?

We're rolling out neighborhood by neighborhood. Drop your address or ZIP into the checker and we'll tell you instantly, or add you to the waitlist for your street.

What if you miss a pickup?

It's rare, but if a crew ever misses your cans, that week is on us, automatically credited back. Our on-time rate is 99%+.

Where do you put the cans back?

Wherever you keep them, side of the house, garage, behind the gate. You tell us once during sign-up and we return them to the same spot every time.
